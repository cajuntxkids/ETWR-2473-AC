LeBron James

LeBron James, born December 30, 1984, is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players in NBA history. Known for his athleticism, leadership, and versatility on the court, LeBron has won multiple NBA championships, MVP awards, and has broken countless records. His career includes time with the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers.
