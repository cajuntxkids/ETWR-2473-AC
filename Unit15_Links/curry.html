Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry, born March 14, 1988, is renowned for revolutionizing basketball with his remarkable shooting ability and range. As a key player for the Golden State Warriors, Curry has won multiple NBA championships, set numerous 3-point shooting records, and earned MVP titles. His impact on the game has popularized a new era of three-point shooting in basketball.
