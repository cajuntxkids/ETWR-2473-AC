James Harden

James Harden, born August 26, 1989, is a prolific NBA shooting guard known for his offensive prowess and unique playing style. With his signature step-back three-pointer and creative ball handling, Harden has redefined scoring in the modern NBA. Over his career, he's claimed multiple scoring titles and established himself as one of the league's most impactful players.
